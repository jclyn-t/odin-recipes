Iced Matcha Latte Recipe

Description

Iced Matcha Latte takes just minutes to make but will keep you energized all day long. This creamy and refreshing green tea powder drink is packed with antioxidants, too.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup ice cubes
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 1-2 tablespoons simple syrup
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon matcha
  • 2 tablespoons hot water

Steps

  1. Gather all the ingredients
  2. To a tall glass, add 1 cup ice cubes and 3/4 cup milk
  3. If you're sweetening your drink, add 1-2 tablespoons homemade simple syrup. Set aside
  4. Into a bowl with a pouring spout, add 1½ teaspoon matcha and 2 tablespoons hot water
  5. Using a bamboo whisk, whisk briskly back and forth, drawing a letter W. Mix until no lumps remain, about 20 seconds. The matcha mixture should be smooth and a little foamy. If it‘s not fluid, you can add a tiny bit of water (a teaspoon at a time) until it‘s a pourable consistency. Then, pour the matcha mixture into the ice-filled milk glass.
  6. Stir and enjoy!
