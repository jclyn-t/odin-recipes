Sukiyaki Recipe

Description

Sukiyaki is one of the most popular hotpot dishes in Japan! All the ingredients, beef, shiitake, ito konnyaku, napa cabbage, yaki dofu make the broth more delicious.

Ingredients

  • 300g thin beef slices
  • 1 napa cabbage leaf
  • 1 long green onion
  • 4 shiitake mushrooms
  • 100g enoki mushrooms
  • 100g pan fried tofu
  • 100g konnyaku noodles
  • 2-4 eggs
  • 100ml sake
  • 50ml mirin
  • 50ml soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons sugar

Steps

  1. First, let's make the sukiyaki sauce also known as Warishita. In a pot, combine the sake, mirin, soy sauce and sugar. Adding the sake makes the beef more delicious. Mirin is similar to sake but it has more flavor and sweetness.
  2. Bring the sauce to a boil and simmer it for a while to let the alcohol evaporate completely. Now, the sauce is ready.
  3. Cut the beef slices in half.
  4. Cut out the firm white part from the napa cabbage leaves. Thinly slice the white part using diagonal cuts. Cut the leafy part into smaller pieces.
  5. Remove the stem of the shiitake mushroom. Carve a star-shaped pattern on the caps of the shiitake.
  6. Slice the yaki dofu, grilled firm tofu into bite-size pieces.
  7. Slice the long green onion also known as naganegi using diagonal cuts.
  8. Parboil the ito konnyaku or shirataki noodles and strain them thoroughly. Then, cut into smaller pieces.
  9. Now, all the preparations are finished. Let's make the sukiyaki.
  10. First, sautee the beef slices in a pan. Then, ladle the sukiyaki sauce over the beef.
  11. Next, add the napa cabbage leaves, yaki dofu, grilled firm tofu and ito konnyaku. Then, add the enoki mushrooms and long green onion.
  12. Pour additional sukiyaki sauce over the vegetables. You can adjust the amount of sukiyaki sauce as you test it. Cover with a lid and simmer for a couple of more minutes. Now, the sukiyaki is ready.
